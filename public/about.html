About Us

What is Mang Inamerz?

Mang Inamerz is about two things: authenticity and transformation. It's about 9 chefs from different cities in the Philippines, who have come together to bring you their authentic versions of Filipino delicacies − they say it's the best they've ever had, we say give us more. Mang Inamerz is also about turning tradition on its head. There will be no waiters in white uniforms, no starchy ambiance, and no eye-watering prices. Instead you'll get fast service in an informal tapas setting with prices that won't break the bank. And with your help, Mang Inamerz can bring this unique dining experience to the people of Manila.

Our Mission

To take Filipino dining experience to the next-level of taste and innovation. Mang Inamerz will turn the dining experience upside down, and make every meal a deliciously sustainable one.
